Claire Durst, a Maryland native, graduated Drew University in 2015. There she obtained a Bachelor's Degree in Sociology with a minor in Arts Administration, with a focus on nonprofit management. Claire also served on the University admissions department as a tour guide and sang in multiple choirs.

Afer college, Claire served a year of service in the AmeriCorps NCC (National Civilian Community Corps) in her home town. There she worked with the nonprofit Rebuilding Together Montgomery County. Her main duty was to repair low-income homes and help other groups in the area to serve their community. Since her term ended she has decided to take on a new skill, coding.
